The Way Forward

Since opening our very first Sandals Resort in 1981, you, our loyal returning guests have been our inspiration and driving force. It is to that end that we strive to bring you the most value for your loyalty leading us to expand, innovate, and create new experiences across the brand. As we enter the new year, we are committed to enhancing the loyalty program to offer you the best value as cherished members of our Sandals and Beaches family.

Peter Menges,

Senior Vice President Loyalty and Customer Experience

Enjoy The Perks Of Paradise

As the World’s only 5-Star Luxury Included® Resorts, we’ve made it easier than ever to experience the benefits of returning to Sandals® and Beaches® Resorts. With exclusive perks, offers and a simple tier system, you can always expect a rewarding escape to paradise.

  • 5,000 Sandals Select Bonus Points Upon Enrollment
  • A token of our appreciation in the form of Sandals Select points for each return visit, up to 1,250 bonus points added to your account
  • Access to Official Sandals Select Loyalty Facebook Group
  • Invitation to Member Appreciation Dinners During Your Stay
  • We recognize our VIP's with special events during your visit
  • Everyone loves a FREE week vacation and we love to give our members just that
  • Members receive up to 20% off at the Red Lane Spa, Photo or Gift Shop
